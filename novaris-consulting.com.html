About Us

Who We Are

A dedicated consulting firm committed to data accuracy, consistency, and quality at every level of your organization.

Novaris Consulting is a forward-thinking consulting firm specializing in Data Quality and Data Governance solutions. We help businesses eliminate data errors, improve consistency, and establish governance frameworks that drive confident decision-making.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive data solutions designed to ensure your information is accurate, consistent, and ready for action.

📊

Data Quality Analysis

Deep analysis of your data sets to identify errors, inconsistencies, and anomalies. We provide detailed reports with actionable insights to improve overall data health.

✅

Data Verification

Thorough cross-checking and validation of data entries to ensure accuracy. Multiple verification rounds to eliminate mistakes before data reaches final use.

🏛️

Data Governance

Establishing structured policies and processes for data management. We help define ownership, quality standards, and workflows to maintain long-term data integrity.

🧹

Data Cleaning & Validation

Identifying and correcting corrupt, incomplete, or inaccurate records. Our systematic cleaning process ensures your databases are reliable and ready for use.
